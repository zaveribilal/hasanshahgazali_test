About

Hazrat Shaykh Hasan Shah Gazali(R.A), also known as Shahenshah-e-Konkan





Mazar Shareef of Sajjada Nashin Haji Mohammed Ali Gazali (R.A).

HAZRAT SHAYKH HASAN SHAH GAZALI IS THE ELDEST BROTHER OF HAZRAT MOMIN SHAH GAZALI (R.A) AND HAZRAT ISMAIL SHAH GAZALI (R.A). THEY CAME TO INDIA FROM BAGHDAD MORE THAN 900 YRS BEFORE VIA SEA.





THEY ARE DIRECT DESCENDANTS OF HAZRAT SYED IMAM GAZALI (R.A)'S YOUNGER BROTHER HAZRAT SYED AHMED GAZALI (R.A). ALL THE BROTHERS BELONG TO THE NAQSHBANDI SILSILA OF SUFISIM


"The URS of Hazrat Shaykh Hasan Shah Gazali (R.A) and Hazrat Mohammed Ali Gazali (R.A) is Celebrated on 12th & 13th of Jamadil Akhir."
